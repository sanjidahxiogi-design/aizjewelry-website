OEM/ODM Bulk Customization Path

Integrating professional 3D engineering with industrial-scale manufacturing.

01

Advanced 3D & CAD Design

We transform your sketches or ideas into precision blueprints using advanced 3D modeling. This modern approach ensures perfect geometry and structural balance before any material is cast.

02

Digital Prototype Approval

You review and confirm the 3D digital model. This step is critical in bulk customization to prevent errors and ensure the final physical sample matches your exact brand vision.

03

Master Molding & Mass Production

After digital approval, we proceed to physical molding and batch production. Our facility is optimized for bulk orders, ensuring consistency and quality across every single piece.

Important Notes for Custom Projects

Final Confirmation Policy

Please double-check the 3D & CAD files carefully. Once the master mold is opened, structural changes are not possible.

Intellectual Property Protection

Your designs are safe with us. We maintain strict confidentiality and exclusive production rights for your brand's assets.

Technical Tolerances

While our 3D engineering is precise, mass production allows for standard industrial tolerances in weight and dimension (±5%).

Batch Sampling

Custom 3D modeling and sampling typically take 7-12 days. Fees are generally deductible upon bulk order placement.

Commitment to Sustainability

Building a cleaner future for the jewelry industry through responsible manufacturing.

Responsible Sourcing

We prioritize the use of recycled 925 Sterling Silver and high-grade 316L Stainless Steel. Our materials are traceable and sourced from suppliers who adhere to strict environmental standards.

Eco-Friendly Finishing

Our PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating and plating processes are designed to be non-toxic and low-emission. We utilize closed-loop water filtration systems to minimize chemical waste.

Ethical Artisanry

We provide a safe, fair, and healthy working environment for our skilled artisans. Sustainability to us means investing in the people who bring your jewelry designs to life.
