Frequently Asked Questions

Can you customize the finish?

Yes. Solid gold can be produced in yellow, white, or rose tones, then finished with mirror, brushed, matte, hammered, or sandblasted textures.

What is the MOQ and lead time?

MOQ is 1 pc per style and production starts from 2 weeks, which is suitable for custom fine-jewelry orders and small collections.

Can I start from sketches or samples?

Yes. You can provide sketches, samples, reference photos, or CAD data, and we can work from the material path that fits the project.

Can I confirm branding details before production?

Yes. We can align the final polish level, structural details, and packaging direction before production.
