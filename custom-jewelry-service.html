Custom Jewelry Services — FAQ

Straight answers to the questions brands ask us most.

What is your MOQ for custom jewelry?

It depends on the material: Solid K Gold (9K / 14K / 18K) starts from just 1 piece, S925 Sterling Silver from 30 pieces per style, and Stainless Steel (SUS304 / SUS316) from 100 pieces per style. That makes us a genuinely low MOQ jewelry manufacturer for independent brands and startups.

Can you manufacture custom jewelry from a sketch or reference image?

Yes. Send sketches, reference photos, or a rough idea — our in-house team converts them into precise 3D CAD models for your approval before any production begins. This custom-jewelry-from-sketch service is included in every project.

Do you offer both OEM and ODM jewelry manufacturing?

Yes. As an OEM jewelry manufacturer we produce your original designs under your brand; as an ODM jewelry manufacturer we develop new designs from your brief. Private label and white label programs are both supported.

What costs are included in a custom jewelry quote?

Four parts: 3D design (included free), a one-time mold development fee per style, a sample fee, and the per-piece production price which decreases with quantity. Every line is itemized before production starts.

Will my jewelry designs be kept confidential?

Yes. Your designs belong exclusively to you — we never reproduce client designs for any third party, and a signed NDA is available on request.

Which countries do you work with?

We serve brands, designers, and wholesalers worldwide — including the United States, Spain and wider Europe, the United Arab Emirates and the Middle East, Japan, and Korea. Communication is in English, with a guaranteed response within 12 hours on business days.
